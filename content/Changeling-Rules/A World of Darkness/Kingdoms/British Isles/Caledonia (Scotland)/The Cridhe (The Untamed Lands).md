## The Cridhe (The Untamed Lands)

This unclaimed part of Caledonia includes The Highlands, Troissachs, and Shetland Isles. Though [[Changeling|changelings]] certainly dwell within these parts, none of them recognizes or owes fealty to the feudal structure of [[Changeling|changeling]] society since the return.

### The Highlands

A number of fae clans occupy the Highlands, close-knit communities of blood kin who owe allegiance to none but themselves and the other clans. The lairds of the clans control the known [[Trods|trods]] in the [[Cridhe]], or “heart” of Caledonia, and prevent any of the returning [[Sidhe]] from gaining a foothold. This area also includes the mysterious Loch Ness. The [[Boggans|boggan]] Seamus and his sons Hugh and Rory lead Clan Wrath, and are among the most outspoken of the clan chiefs. Glen Coe, in the Western Highlands, is a favorite meeting place for the [[Shadow Court]], though even they do not linger there.

### The Troissachs

Including parts of central Caledonia and Fife, this realm consists of the meeting place between the Highlands and the Lowlands and the land of Rob Roy. It is also the place where the Kingdoms of Alba and Dalriadha cross territory with the fae clans of the Highlands. Volatile with long held passions, the land seems eager for something.
