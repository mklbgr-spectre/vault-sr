## The Galacian Confederation

This large territory covers much of Europe and contains 90% commoners. In stark contrast to Neustria, the [[Changeling|changelings]] of the Confederation make up most of the rulers of the lands, which include: the Elbian Protectorate (northwest), The Bavarian League, The Thuringian-Saxon Union, the Pomeranian Alliance (north), The Council of White Mountains (Switzerland), and the Province of Venezia. The current head of the Confederation is the [[Satyrs|satyr]] Willie Ems, who makes his [[Freeholds|freehold]] in Bern, Switzerland.
## The Elbian Protectorate

Comprising the Czech Republic, Central Bohemia, Saxony, Brandenburg, Mecklenburg, and Hamburg, the Elbian Protectorate follows the Elbe River basin and contains some of Europe’s most fertile farmlands. Much [[Changeling|changeling]] traffic is by river boat, rather than auto or train. Ruled by an elected counsel headed by Ludvig Magnus, a [[Trolls|troll]] educated in law and economics, this land enjoys an openness lacking in many places ruled by nobles. Any nobles who visit must be on their best behavior. Ludvig’s [[Freeholds|freehold]], Trollhaven, is in Hamburg, Germany.

## The Bavarian League
Located in the southeastern part of Germany, noted for its beautiful forests (the Bavarian and the Bohemian), as well as the Bavarian Alps, this beautiful land offers much for commoner [[Changeling|changelings]] and is wary toward visiting [[Sidhe]]. Governed by a congress made up of every commoner kith, the League answers to a Prime Minister, currently the [[Satyrs|satyr]] Lorelei Gutwald.

## The Thuringian-Saxon Union
Called the “Green Heart” of Germany, Thuringia is a land of mountains and splendid forests. Saxony, which borders Thuringia, has many mountainous regions. The two areas have found common interests in both dramatic environments and growing technologies. [[Nockers|Nockers]] interested in microchip technology flock to this region. While [[Changeling|changelings]] of all commoner kiths live here, [[Nockers|nockers]] have a little more say than most. The current ruler of this parliamentary government is Gustav Mach, a wilder [[Nockers|nocker]] whose dreams are very modern.

## The Pomeranian Alliance
Located on the southern shore of the Baltic Sea, the Pomeranian Alliance rules much of northern Germany and the Baltic States of Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania. A loose confederation of realms that caters to [[Boggans|boggans]], [[Nockers|nockers]], and [[Trolls|trolls]], with some [[Redcaps|redcaps]], the Pomeranian Alliance has tried to ban [[Sidhe]] altogether, but has not been entirely successful. Few nobles visit, however. The [[Boggans|boggan]] Josef the Worthy currently holds the leadership of the Alliance. The elected representatives meet monthly at his [[Freeholds|freehold]] in the city of Gdansk.

## The Council of White Mountains
This land consists of the Alpine regions of Austria, Switzerland, France, and Italy. [[Changeling|Changelings]] from all over the world come here yearly for the skiing, and this is the only time nobles are tolerated. The [[Trolls|troll]] Benito Franci currently heads the council, which consists of representatives from all the Alpine regions.

## The Province of Venezia
This realm includes the city of Venice and surrounding areas. While commoners rule here, [[Sidhe]] are welcome as visitors, but rarely as residents. Drawn by its annual Carnival, its picturesque scenery, and the phenomenon of a city of islands with canals as its roads draw many [[Changeling|changeling]] and mortal tourists. Currently a [[Nockers|nocker]], Julieta di Falco leads the ruling council.
