## Kingdom of Alba

This kingdom includes Aberdeenshire, Central, Tayside, Fife, and Grampian — the Lowland areas. The cities of Inverness, Perth, Dundee, and Aberdeen comprise the largest cities in the kingdom. King Niall ap [[Dougal]] rules his land with fairness, but lately has become obsessed with the creations of his [[Nockers|nocker]] engineers, who engineered the voice box that replaces the vocal chords he lost to cancer.

### The Tuath of Chronos

This tuath exists entirely within a faerie mound preserved from the ravages of the [[Shattering]]. Usually, both fae and mortals enter this font of [[Glamour]] only through happenstance, or through the machinations of those who dwell within it.
