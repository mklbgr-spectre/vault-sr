## The Isles - The Orkneys and Shetlands

These islands, with their windswept beauty and harsh living, now enjoy the prosperity of the offshore oil business. Dominated by [[Trolls|trolls]], who answer only to their own thanes and jarls, they support the Kingdom of Alba in gratitude for the gift of a magic boat which was crafted for their acknowledged leader, Asa, by one of King Niall’s artificers.
