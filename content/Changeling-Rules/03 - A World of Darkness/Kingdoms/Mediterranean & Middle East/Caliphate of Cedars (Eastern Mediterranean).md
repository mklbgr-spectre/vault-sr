## Caliphate of Cedars

The Caliphate of Cedars includes the countries once known as the Levant, or “The Land Where the Sun Rises”: Lebanon, Syria, Jordan, and Israel. Many [[Changeling|changelings]] have come from Europe, drawn by the mild climate and beautiful scenery. The political turmoil of the region feeds the [[Glamour]] of some of the [[Kithain]], notably the [[Redcaps|redcaps]] and some of the [[Trolls|trolls]]. Most, however, strive to maintain a graceful lifestyle, with touches of Arabian and Turkish influences in dress and manners. The current caliph, the [[Pooka|pooka]] Mahbub ibn Iqbal (“Beloved Son of Fortune”) keeps his court and any visitors entertained with stories equaled only by those of the legendary Scheherazade. The frequent unrest in the region has driven many [[Changeling|changelings]] back to Europe, but some stubborn few have vowed to preserve the dreams of the region’s ancient cultures.
