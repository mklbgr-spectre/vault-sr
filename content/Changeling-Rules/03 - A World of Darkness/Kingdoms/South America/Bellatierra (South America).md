Called "the beautiful land" by early explorers and modern-day adventurers, the lands of South America have not proven altogether friendly to changelings. Only a few areas have come to the attention of the fae of [[Concordia]] and of Europe. The rest of the countries, particularly those in the vast jungle interior, remain places of caution. Changelings have heard of the [[Kingdom of Amazonia]], which encompasses the vast country of Brazil, and the Mountain Kingdoms, which include Chile and Peru.
