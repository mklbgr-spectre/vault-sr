## Kingdom of Flowers

Ruled by King Picolette of House [[Gwydion]], the Kingdom of Flowers includes all of the Netherlands. Commoners find a welcome here from the court of King Picolette so long as they have only peaceful intentions.

## The Duchy of Tulips

This duchy includes the city of Amsterdam, with its rich heritage and cosmopolitan atmosphere. Duke Gwilliam of House [[Liam]] rules the Court of Tulips, the city’s largest [[Freeholds|freehold]]. He particularly welcomes commoners, especially those who are disenchanted with the complex politics of [[Concordia]].
