Hsien use the following traits, some of which replace those inapplicable to the Shinma, others of which provide additional dimensions inapplicable to changelings.

## Shentao

Much like Kenning, which it replaces, Shentao represents the innate magical sense common to the Shinma. It allows them to sense each other, dragon nests, and dragon lines, and to detect those who hunt them. Shentao may be used to assess changelings at a +2 difficulty. Kithain suffer a similar difficulty when using Kenning on Shinma.

- Novice: You feel it when you wake up after having slept on a dragon line.
- Practiced: You have no trouble locating dragon nests or True Jade.
- Competent: You're able to reliably spot other Hsien, regardless of whether they want to be found.
- Expert: You make a pastime of people watching and picking out which people are mortals and which very much aren't.
- Master: Nothing is hidden.

**Possessed by:** Hsien, other shen, very aware mortals.

**Specialties:** Dragon Nests, Dragon Lines, Hsien, Hengeyo-kai, Kuei-jin, Shih, Chi'n Ta, True Jade, Ghosts, Spirits, Demons.
