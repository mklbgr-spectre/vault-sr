# Player Characters

All four are **Knight Errant** rank members of the [[Knights of the Chalice]].

---

## Fariel (Aldren Valior) — The Detached Observer

- **Race:** Elf
- **Player:** Clemens

**Personality:** Emotionally detached and highly analytical. Mimics emotions rather than feeling them. Sees life as a philosophical journey, influenced by elven beliefs about reincarnation.

**Backstory:** Woke up years ago with no memories before his university days. Has since been financially supported by an unknown benefactor who subtly controls aspects of his life from the shadows. He has experimented with emotions, relationships, and psychoactive substances in an attempt to understand human experience, but remains an outsider to it. Has a strong interest in astral phenomena and metaplanar exploration.

> [!warning] GM Hook
> The unknown benefactor is an active dangling thread. Who are they, and how do they connect to the larger plot?

**Magic & Abilities:** Illusion and subtle magic, particularly mind-affecting spells.

**Metaplanar Trial:** Mirrors and identity — forced to confront whether his control over himself is truly self-determined or shaped by external forces.

---

## Hamish O'Donnell — The Spirit Pariah

- **Race:** Gnome
- **Player:** Alex

**Personality:** Witty, intelligent, and ambitious, but harbors deep frustration over his inability to connect with spirits. Holds an unwavering belief in his noble bloodline, though he struggles to prove it. Tends to overanalyse situations, which can slow him down when action is needed.

**Backstory:** Raised in Scotland by his mother, who told him he was the son of a powerful noble. When she died, he pursued his lineage but found only mysteries. Despite excelling in magic, he is cursed as a **Spirit Pariah** — spirits avoid him entirely. This fuels his obsession with barrier magic; he prefers protection and control over summoning.

**Magic & Abilities:** Defensive magic, counterspelling, and barrier creation.

**Metaplanar Trial:** Confronted by spirits using barriers against him — forced to assert his own power rather than seek validation from others.

---

## Willow — The Trickster of the Wild

- **Race:** Pixie
- **Player:** Simi

**Personality:** Playful, clever, and mischievous, but deeply connected to nature. Loves shapeshifting and interacting with animals. A natural trickster with a kind heart — careful never to take things too far.

**Backstory:** Studied at [[Avalon University]], where she built a vast network of magical contacts. Despite her whimsical nature, she has a strong sense of curiosity and enjoys testing the limits of reality.

**Magic & Abilities:** Shapeshifting and illusion.

**Metaplanar Trial:** A spirit-fox leads her through a shifting maze — she must use transformation magic to outmaneuver a guardian stag.

---

## Shane Hunt — The Spear of Legacy

- **Race:** Satyr
- **Player:** Kiki

**Personality:** Direct, strong-willed, and determined. Combat-focused adept. Dislikes political maneuvering but has learned to navigate corporate entanglements. Driven by the need to define her own identity outside her father's shadow.

**Backstory:** Daughter of a former Saeder-Krupp executive who was later revealed to be a double agent for an unknown faction. She grew up with privilege but now carries the burden of his betrayal. Fiercely independent as a result.

> [!warning] GM Hook
> Shane's father was a double agent — for whom? Unresolved thread.

**Magic & Abilities:** Physical adept, specialising in direct combat and enhanced reflexes.

**Metaplanar Trial:** A series of gruelling physical challenges leading to a radiant spear — forging her own legacy outside her father's shadow.

---

## Relay (Nadia Safiya) — The Spirit-Touched Guide

*GM character and storytelling device*

- **Race:** Human
- **Player:** Mike (GM)

**Personality:** Thoughtful, spiritually attuned, and deeply tied to ancient Egyptian magical traditions. Carries a subtle guilt for not following her parents' Islamic traditions as strictly as they would like.

**Backstory:** Born in Egypt to middle-income, non-corporate parents. Her magical potential was sensed before she was born. Her awakening happened at age 5 when she spoke with a spirit resembling an ancient pharaoh at a museum — drawing the attention of Egyptian magical scholars. This led to her sponsorship by the Egyptian magical order and later her recruitment into the Knights of the Chalice.

**Personality Quirk:** Severe phobia of guns. As a child (~14 years old), she was caught in a firefight between terrorists and police on a train — trapped on the floor, unable to move, watching the chaos around her. The experience left a permanent mark. Her gun phobia remains after the metaplanar trial, but has less power over her than before.

**Magic & Abilities:** Spirit possession, channelling divine energies, and exorcism. She willingly allows spirits to possess her, serving as a conduit rather than a direct spellcaster.

**Metaplanar Trial:** The trial manifested as a shifting desert caught between ancient Egypt and her Islamic childhood. At its centre: the train. She was forced to re-enter the memory and guide her younger self to safety — fighting through the armed spirits using magic, refusing to pick up a gun despite the opportunity. Her younger self dissolved into warmth once freed. The trial ended with a spirit merging into her, representing acceptance of both sides of her identity. The GM read the train scene aloud in German to the table during Arc 2.
