# Metamagic — Psychometry

> [!warning] SR5 Source Material
> This document draws entirely from **Shadowrun 5th Edition** sources: *Street Grimoire* (2014) and *Forbidden Arcana* (2017). The campaign uses **Shadowrun Anarchy**, which does not have a direct equivalent mechanic. This reference is provided so the GM can adapt or port the ability for use in the Anarchy system. Treat all dice pool mechanics, limits, and drain references as SR5 constructs requiring GM conversion.

---

## Overview

Psychometry is a metamagic technique within the **Psychometry High Art** — one of several schools of initiatory magic alongside Geomancy, Necromancy, and Divination. It is the magical ability to read the emotional and sensory impressions left on objects, places, and people by past events. Those who master it can reconstruct scenes, identify individuals, and perceive emotional histories from physical contact with an item.

> [!note] In-World Context
> The ability mirrors what the Sixth World calls a "sixth sense" pseudoscience — but is real, reliable magic when practiced by an Awakened initiate. Oxford Royal College of Magicians is explicitly cited as a school that teaches advanced psychometry, training arcanoarchaeologists and curators to identify fakes and trace grave robbers. This fits directly with the Knights of the Chalice's investigative remit.

## Prerequisites

### Initiation Requirement
Psychometry is a metamagic. Each new metamagic technique requires the character to initiate to the next grade to learn it (SR5 core, p. 325). There is no minimum initiation grade stated beyond the standard metamagic acquisition rules.

### Prerequisite: Sympathetic Linking
> "Those who initiate into psychometry usually first learn **Sympathetic Linking** for rituals."
> — *Street Grimoire*, p. 144

Sympathetic Linking is the prerequisite metamagic. A character must know it before learning Psychometry.

### School Membership (High Arts Context)
Psychometry is acquired as part of initiating into the Psychometry school of High Arts. When a magician first initiates into a High Art school, they learn one available ritual, enchantment, or metamagic for free (barring prerequisites). Additional metamagics within the school require initiating to the next grade.

> [!note] Rituals and Enchanting in the Psychometry School
> The school sidebar (p. 144) lists the following school resources:
> - **Enchanting:** Symbolic Link, Mystic Restraints
> - **Metamagics:** Sympathetic Linking, Psychometry
>
> These enchantments and metamagics are **only available** to those initiated into this school. They cannot be used by other magicians unless they have also initiated into Psychometry.

## What Psychometry Does — Full Rules Text

### The Experience (Flavour and Nature)

> "Psychometry is like experiencing flashbacks, a flood of senses and emotions that can be uncontrollable. Once the magician develops this skill, the door can be difficult to close. Everything the magician touches generates this kind of feedback. Do you really need to know the life story behind every shot glass and barstool? The magician learns to put up mental shields to filter out distractions from all the input he would get from astral imprints."
> — *Street Grimoire*, p. 145

Objects retain psychic imprints on the astral plane — echoes of the emotions and events associated with them. Psychometry allows the magician to intentionally read these imprints. The ability is pervasive and requires active mental filtering during ordinary life.

### How Objects Retain Impressions

> "Buildings that had some significance to people will still retain some astral shadow, even if the building has been torn down. This is the same for objects that can be used as a sympathetic link. The objects don't gain an aura like living things, but they do resonate with emotions and impressions. Emotions can be associated with objects, similar to the way a sound in the astral world is comparable to the colors of an aura."
> — *Street Grimoire*, p. 144

## Mechanical Procedure

### Step 1 — Raise or Lower Mental Shields
The magician needs only **a few moments** to raise or lower her shields to attempt reading an object. This is a preparatory mental action, not a full action.

### Step 2 — Make the Test
**Test:** `Assensing + Intuition + initiate grade [Astral]`

Consult the **Psychometry Table** for situational modifiers and results (see table below).

### Step 3 — Duration of the Reading
The magician **cannot stop** the reading once it begins and has **no control over how long it takes**. The gamemaster rolls **1D6** to choose the duration, or decides based on the psychometric experience being appropriate to the scene:
- 1 minute
- 5 minutes
- Longer (GM discretion)

### Step 4 — Dice Pool Penalty During Reading
While the reading is ongoing, the magician suffers a **–2 dice pool penalty to all other actions**.

## The Psychometry Table

*(Street Grimoire, p. 146)*

### Time Since Impression

| Time Since Impression | Modifier |
|---|---|
| 1 day | +0 |
| 1 week | –2 |
| 1 month | –4 |
| 1 year | –6 |
| 1 decade | –8 |
| Each additional decade | –2 |

> [!note] Underground / Isolated Locations
> Items isolated from other auras or from being disturbed (tombs, basements, abandoned towns) can keep their impression for much longer. It is suggested that items within the Gaiasphere (underground) retain impressions longer. The GM can reduce the time modifier to that of a week, month, or year for these kinds of items.

### Nature of Item

| Situation | Modifier |
|---|---|
| Nature of item has a strong connection to the impression (wedding ring, beloved toy, etc.) | +2 |
| Initiate had a strong connection to item when impression was formed (friend's toy, school building, etc.) | +2 |
| Impression recorded violence or powerful emotions | +3 |
| Impression recorded violent death | +4 |
| Item is a bonded focus | + (Rating)** |
| Item is a place of more than 500 square kilometers | –2 |
| Item carries more than one significant impression | –1 for each |
| Astral signature has been erased (p. 312, SR5) | –1 |
| Cleansing metamagic has been used — Smudging (p. 136, SG) | –Force |
| Each subsequent attempt to read the item | –2 |

> [!note] Bonded Focus Modifier
> The Rating bonus applies only to impressions related to the person to whom the focus is bonded. Stacked foci only grant the highest rating as a bonus.

### Information Gained (by Hits)

| Hits | Information Gained |
|---|---|
| 0 | None |
| 1 | Flashes of insight, superficial and disjointed impressions |
| 2 | Longer and deeper insights, though visions are still disjointed |
| 3 | Greater details, multiple sensory feedback, more coherence |
| 4+ | Lengthy sequences, multiple senses, coherent visions, significant information |

## What Psychometry Can Reveal

The reading surfaces **emotional and sensory impressions** left on an object, not a literal recording. Results are interpreted through the tradition and symbolism of the magician:

- **1 hit:** Vague feelings, fleeting images, disconnected emotions
- **2 hits:** More sustained impressions — mood, general sense of events, partial imagery
- **3 hits:** Multi-sensory detail — sounds, smells, visual sequences, emotional context
- **4+ hits:** Coherent narrative visions, specific identifying details, significant information about events or persons

> [!note] Tradition Colouring
> The nature of the vision is filtered through the magician's paradigm. A hermetic mage might see formulae and ordered symbolic constructs; a shaman might experience the impressions as spirit visions or dream sequences. The information content is the same, but its presentation reflects the reader's tradition.

### Items That Carry Impressions Well
- Objects closely tied to strong emotions (wedding rings, weapons used in violence, beloved personal items)
- Objects used frequently and personally by their owner
- Places associated with significant events — including demolished buildings (their astral shadow persists)
- Bonded foci (carry deep impressions of their bonded owner)

### What Psychometry Cannot Do
- It does not allow communication with the dead (that is Necromancy — specifically the Whisper of Bones ritual, p. 136)
- Visions are impressionistic and interpreted, not literal recordings — they can be misread
- The magician cannot selectively choose which impression to read if multiple are present (–1 modifier per additional impression)
- The Smudging ritual (see below) can permanently reduce or erase imprints

## Limitations and Complications

### The Always-On Problem
Once the magician learns Psychometry, every object she touches generates feedback. She must actively maintain mental shields to filter this out in daily life. Lowering those shields to do a deliberate reading takes only moments, but the pervasive nature of the ability is a significant quality-of-life burden.

### Uncontrollable Duration
The magician cannot choose to end a reading early. The GM sets or rolls the duration (1D6, with results of 1 minute, 5 minutes, or longer). This can create vulnerability during operational situations.

### Subsequent Attempts Degrade
Each subsequent attempt to read the same item incurs a **–2 cumulative penalty**. The impressions are not worn out, but the magician's repeated access to the same psychic "signal" becomes harder to cleanly interpret.

### Smudging Degrades Impressions
The **Smudging ritual** (Street Grimoire, p. 136) permanently reduces the psychic imprint of a place or object by the number of net successes of the ritual. It also reduces astral signatures. This is both a counter-measure psychometrists should be aware of, and a tool they may use themselves after reading a crime scene.

> [!warning] Crime Scene Awareness
> A Smudging ritual applied to a scene before the psychometrist arrives will impose a penalty equal to the ritual's Force on the reading. Sophisticated criminals or cover-up operatives may use Smudging specifically to defeat forensic psychometry.

## Sympathetic Link Cross-Reference

Psychometry readings reference objects as **sympathetic links** — the same objects used in ritual magic to target or locate individuals. A few key rules from the Sympathetic Linking section (p. 145–146) that directly affect psychometric practice:

### Sympathetic Links and Their Viability
| Link Type | Duration of Viability |
|---|---|
| Sentimental objects | Weeks |
| Frequently worn items | Days |
| Objects held for 5+ minutes | Up to twice the time held during most recent usage |
| Tissue samples (blood, etc.) | Blood: hours; finger or larger sample: days |

> [!note] Chemical Preservation Destroys Links
> Chemical preservatives destroy the viability of biological material links. A blood sample treated with a forensic preservative cannot be used as a material link — but it can still carry psychometric impressions as an object (impressions are astral, not biological).

## Smudging — Counter to Psychometry

*(Street Grimoire, p. 136, referenced in context of Psychometry on p. 144)*

Smudging is a cleansing ritual that permanently reduces psychic imprints.

> "As noted in Psychometry (p. 144), this ritual permanently reduces the psychic imprint by number of successes for the ritual. Smudging also reduces the existence of all astral signatures by number of successes within the area (p. 312, SR5). The Smudging ritual cleans an area equal to the ([leader's Magic Rating + initiate grade] x Force) in meters. It takes Force x 30 minutes to complete the ritual. **Requires Cleansing.**"
> — *Street Grimoire*, p. 136

| Effect of Smudging on Psychometry |
|---|
| Permanently reduces imprint strength by net successes |
| Modifier to psychometry reading: –Force (from Psychometry Table) |
| Area cleaned: ([Magic + initiate grade] x Force) meters |
| Time to complete: Force x 30 minutes |
| Prerequisite: Cleansing metamagic |

## Cross-References from Forbidden Arcana

### The Seer (Focused Awakened Archetype)
*(Forbidden Arcana, p. 43)*

The Seer is a focused Awakened type theorized to be the earliest manifestation of magic. One of their defining features:

> "Gains psychometry metamagic for free (p. 145, *Street Grimoire*)."

The Seer also gains Sensing metamagic (p. 158, SG) for free, and treats Astral Perception as a Complex Action rather than a Simple Action.

> [!note] GM Note — The Seer and Investigation
> The Seer archetype is a natural fit for the forensic investigator role. A PC or NPC built as a Seer who gains Psychometry for free would be a credible specialist in object reading without requiring a full Psychometry school initiation path.

### Guidance Spirits — Psychometry Spirit Power
*(Street Grimoire Errata, p. 2 — Greater Spirit Powers sidebar correction)*

The errata corrects the Greater Spirit Powers sidebar (originally Street Grimoire p. 115) to give **Guidance spirits** the Psychometry spirit power (p. 144) as their Greater Spirit Power, replacing the original erroneous listing.

> "**Guidance:** Psychometry (p. 144)"
> — *Street Grimoire Errata*, p. 2

This means Guidance spirits, when summoned as Great Form spirits (p. 136, SG — 4 hits on the Great Form Spirit table), gain the ability to use Psychometry. A conjurer could theoretically direct a Great Form Guidance spirit to read an object — though the spirit's results would be interpreted and communicated by the spirit, not experienced directly by the magician.

> [!note] Investigative Application
> This is a significant tool for investigation. A conjurer without Psychometry themselves can access the mechanic via a Great Form Guidance spirit, at the cost of summoning effort and interpretation fidelity.

### Fictional Institutions Teaching Psychometry
- **Oxford Royal College of Magicians** — teaches advanced psychometry; trains arcanoarchaeologists and curators to interpret artifacts, identify fakes, and search for grave robbers
- Psychometry is listed as an accredited field of study in the **American Association for the Advancement of Thaumaturgy (AAAT)** list of Awakened degree fields (*Street Grimoire*, p. 17)

### Profession: Occult Profiler
*(Street Grimoire, p. 144 — sidebar)*

> "While in most cases law enforcement isn't allowed to use psychometric techniques to hunt down criminals, the same rules don't apply for occult profilers or mystic bounty hunters. Provided the price is right, these magicians can track down people by using psychometry, watchers, and rituals, even when the trail has already gone cold for mundane techniques."

### Profession: Forensic Thaumaturgist
*(Street Grimoire, p. 144 — sidebar)*

Forensic thaumaturgists routinely use psychometry at crime scenes. Their Perception and Assensing skills allow them to find astral signatures and review emotional context. The forensic thaumaturgist has tools to find clues mundane investigators cannot access.

## Quick Reference — At the Table

**To read an object:**
1. Lower mental shields (a few moments)
2. Roll `Assensing + Intuition + initiate grade [Astral]`
3. Apply modifiers from the Psychometry Table (time, item nature)
4. GM sets or rolls reading duration (1D6: 1 min / 5 min / longer)
5. Magician suffers –2 to all actions during the reading
6. Interpret results by hits (0 = nothing, 1 = flashes, 2 = deeper, 3 = multi-sensory, 4+ = coherent visions)

**Key vulnerabilities:**
- Smudging ritual erases imprints (–Force to roll)
- Erased astral signature: –1
- Multiple impressions on item: –1 each
- Subsequent attempts: –2 cumulative
- Older impressions: significant negative modifiers

**Best conditions:**
- Fresh impressions (within a day)
- Violent or emotionally charged events (+3 to +4)
- Item strongly connected to the person (+2)
- Bonded focus belonging to subject (+ Rating)
