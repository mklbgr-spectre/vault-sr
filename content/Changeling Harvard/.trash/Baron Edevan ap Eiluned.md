Baron Edevan ap Eiluned Magister Primus of the Crimson Spire 
• Kith: Sidhe 
• House: Eiluned 
• Seeming: Grump 
• Court: Seelie (Autumn-aligned) 

Baron Edevan is the master of the Crimson Spire, a figure of quiet authority and unfathomable intellect. An ancient Sidhe of House Eiluned, he serves as both ruler and philosopher-king of the Freehold, maintaining the balance between tradition, dreaming, and mortal ambition. 

Though he rarely raises his voice, his words carry weight and consequence. Edevan is known for his calm demeanor, his ritual precision, and a fondness for riddles that seem to hold truths long after the moment has passed. He oversees the Spire's affairs with patience, gravitas, and a subtle, scholarly air that reminds many of old-world nobility or a tenured scholar whose reach extends well beyond the lecture hall. 

He is deeply respected by the Court—and treated with a mix of awe and caution by those who serve him. Some say he sees patterns in the Dreaming that others cannot. Others say he simply listens better than anyone else. 

Baron Edevan personally presides over major rites, including the Rite of the Crimson Ink, and is said to know the name of every changeling who has ever been bound to the Spire.
