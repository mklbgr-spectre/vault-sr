# Session 009 — Hunting the Butcher

## Following the Wisp

Armed with the name "The Butcher" from last session's interrogation, Thea cast **Seer's Wisp** (Soothsay 2), producing a literal wisp to trail.

- We followed it on foot at first, then switched to rented scooters
- Direction: southwestward, out of Boston proper, into a forested area on the city's outskirts (roughly toward New York)

---

## The Bonfire

The wisp led us to a bonfire gathering deep in the woods. We held back and observed from stealth.

- **7 people present** — all Ranters, no named individuals (more or less henchmen)
- Anti-noble chants clearly audible
- **The Butcher** addressed the group: their goal was to break out the two Redcaps we captured in Session 8
- We texted **Geoffrey** to loop him in before making any move

---

## The Chase

The Ranters mounted motorcycles and headed out. The original plan was a pincer: **Geoffrey and other freehold members** would hold the freehold and defend the prisoners from the front, while **we came from behind**. For reasons unclear, the engagement began earlier than planned during the highway pursuit — we ended up handling it without the full setup.

We commandeered a pickup truck; Geoffrey joined us for the pursuit.

**Myrrha's contribution on the highway:**

- Cast **Unseen Veil** (Chicanery 2) to keep the truck hidden from the fleeing Ranters
- Bunk: fouled the air inside the vehicle, forcing everyone to make Stamina rolls
- Shameus failed his roll and vomited — fortunately he was riding in the truck bed

**~4/5 of the way**, with Geoffrey's position nearby as potential reinforcement, we went on the offensive:

- **Asher** used **Legerdemain 2** to "steal" a tire from one of the motorcycles mid-chase — the resulting crash took out **4 riders** at once
- **Shameus** used **Wayfare (Hopscotch)** combined with the truck's momentum to launch himself at the 5th rider, knocking them off their bike; took significant damage in the process (~4 Lethal to soak — survived, currently recovering at the freehold)
- **Myrrha** cast **Electrical Gremlins** (Skycraft) on the last motorcycle, taking the final Ranter out of the fight

**Final tally:** 6 Ranters total — **2 dead**, **4 captured**.

**The Butcher** is unaccounted for. At some point during the engagement he escaped via an unidentified spell — the exact Art is unknown, and that's the mystery we're left with.

---

## Court Investigators Arrive

Two investigators showed up on scene to formally take stock:

- **Iawn ap Fiona**
- **Malcolm ap Dougal**

They did the classic procedural handoff — officially assuming oversight of the investigation — but in contrast to the usual trope:

- Asked us to **continue investigating** in parallel and keep them informed
- **Commended the group** for the work done so far
