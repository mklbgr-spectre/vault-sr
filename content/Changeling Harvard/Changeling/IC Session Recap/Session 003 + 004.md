# Session 003 + 004 — The Shadow Wolf

> [!warning] Sparse Notes
> These sessions were not well documented at the time. Only the key facts survive.

---

## The Entrance Trial Concludes

The Near Dreaming forest from the end of Session 002 was not the threat it appeared to be — the plan had been to scare us, nothing more. A joke. A final test.

It went sideways before anyone could execute it: a **Shadow Wolf** attacked the group unprompted.

- We fought it, barely
- We survived
- We looted its **heart**

---

## The Aftermath

The attack was not supposed to happen. The people who set up the trial were as surprised as we were — **Shadow Wolves are not native to this region or this era**. They are old creatures, and their presence here is anomalous.

**Reward**: A small amount of Dross for our trouble.

**Myrrha's mentor** has asked her — and by extension the motley — to look into it further.

---

**SESSION END**

*We passed a test that nearly killed us. Something old is stirring near Boston, and it wasn't supposed to be here.*
