Summary:

Mira (Myrrha) was summoned to the Crimson Spire by a fae cook.

All were briefed by Count Edevan, who assigned them their first task: abducting [[Handsome Dan]] XIX in a symbolic act of inter-academic mischief. He is to be returned safely shortly after the prank.
