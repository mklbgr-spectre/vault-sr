# Session 001 + 002 — Operation Handsome Dan

*In-game timeframe: Sunday through Friday. The group's first assigned task.*

---

## The Briefing

Myrrha was summoned to the **[[crimson spire]]** by a fae cook — no letter, just a summons. The rest of the group received formal invitations.

**[[Count Edevan ap Eiluned]]**, Magister Primus of the Crimson Spire, laid out the mission himself: steal **[[Handsome Dan]] XIX**, Yale's mascot bulldog, ahead of the Yale Parade on Friday. Return him unharmed shortly afterward. The stated purpose is a symbolic act of inter-academic mischief — a prank with fae prestige attached to it.

*Unspoken subtext: this is also a competence test.*

---

## The Plan

**Target window**: Friday morning, before the Yale Parade. Handsome Dan has a scheduled grooming appointment.

**Core approach**: **Asher** impersonates the substitute groomer and intercepts the dog before the real handler can intervene.

- **Thea** used **Soothsay 1 (Omen)** to determine the real groomer's arrival time: **13:30**. Asher plans to be there 30 minutes earlier.
- The real groomer was diverted via a spoofed email made to look like it came from Kassie, instructing her to come to Kassie's home address instead of the stadium.
- **Liam** poses as a campus security officer. **Myrrha** cast **Chicanery 1** to bolster his disguise, adding +4 successes.
- **Thea** placed a **Soothsay (Tattletale)** on the handler's wallet to track her movements remotely.

**Logistics secured**: getaway vehicle, dog crate (a banana box), dog-safe sedative treats, safe location for Dan's brief Harvard retreat.

---

## The Handler

**Kassandra "Kassie" Haro**
Age 25 — Yale Visitor Center staff, Yale alum (Class of '18)
Political science major, Naval ROTC background, former tour guide
Lives with Handsome Dan near the New Haven Green

---

## Execution Day

A final planning conversation was interrupted by the appearance of **[[Arwynn]]** — dark, sardonic, unmistakably Unseelie. He announced, without much concern, that he had placed a bet on our success. Inspiring.

On site at the stadium:

- **Liam** held the parking lot perimeter in his security guard cover
- **Asher** slipped into the grooming appointment without a hitch — and had to pose for a selfie with Kassie and Dan as part of the formalities
- In the backstage grooming room, Asher quietly stole Kassie's phone, deleted the selfie, and returned it before anything was noticed
- **Thea** accessed the stadium PA system and broadcast a fake vandalism alert for Kassie's car — she left, Asher was alone with the dog
- The sedated treats went down without a fight. Dan was asleep within minutes.
- Dog in the banana box, out to the vehicle.

---

## The Escape

Barely clear of the stadium, **Thea** detected a **scrying spell** targeting Handsome Dan. A car was on us — three Yale Boggans, giving chase.

A tense sequence through New Haven traffic ended at a red light. Good rolls, a bit of luck, and the pursuers put themselves into a light fender-bender trying to keep up. They lost us.

---

## The Reward

Back in Cambridge. A pub. The mission was a success.

**[[Arwynn]]** appeared again — confirmed to be a Knight of House Fiona.
**Brychan ap Gwydion**, Seneschal to Count Edevan, arrived shortly after and delivered the reward: **2 Dross per person**.

We also learned that Count Edevan is presently very busy — photographing Handsome Dan against Harvard backdrops for what can only be described as an aggressive social media campaign.

---

## The Near Dreaming

The celebration took a turn. Three Satyrs joined the pub. Magic bled into the air. Things blurred.

We came to in darkness — a forest clearing, unmistakably the **Near Dreaming**. Only our fae mien visible. In the center of the clearing: a single tree with **turquoise leaves**.

Attached to Liam or Finja (unclear who): a handwritten message.

**"This is the second part of your entrance trial."**

We took stock. Survival rolls to orient. Cantrips to pick up the pace. Somewhere behind us in the dark, things were howling.

---

**SESSION END**

*The prank was a success. The trial, apparently, was not over.*
