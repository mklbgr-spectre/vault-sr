# Session 010 — Albert Hoffman

*Picks up the same evening as the end of Session 009.*

---

## Deputized by the Grey Hand

Before splitting off to investigate, the group received formal recognition from **Iawn ap Fiona** and **Malcolm ap Dougal** — the two court investigators from the previous night. The GM described it as being "gethaned": officially deputized by the Grey Hand, granting us sanctioned standing in the Ranter investigation. Somewhere between a commendation and a field commission — roughly halfway to squire/noble status in practical terms.

---

## Two Tracks

With six Ranter dwellings to search and limited time before the mundane police make the dead ranters' apartments inaccessible, the group splits.

### Computer Research — Asher & Thea

Back at base, **Thea** runs a computer search and surfaces an article connecting one of the living Ranters to an HOA in a specific Boston neighborhood. **Asher's** contact attempts to hack the DMV for driving license data on **Alexander Blaskowitz**, one of the Ranters, in hopes of getting a home address.

- *Both the HOA connection and the Blaskowitz thread remain open — their full significance is unclear IC for now.*

### The Punk Bar — Myrrha & Finja

My thinking: the Rusty Nail was where we first picked up Ranter leads. With fresh captures to work from, someone at that bar might know more. **Finja** and I head back to the harbor district.

- Finja works the barkeeper — gets him drinking, keeps him charmed
- He opens up about the Ranter crowd and eventually agrees to take us somewhere
- I text **Thea** to keep her informed of our location as we go

The barkeeper leads us out of the city to a private biker club — a run-down hovel outside of town. We are very clearly in the middle of an active criminal motorcycle gang.

**Myrrha's move**: Cast **Veiled Eyes** *(Chicanery 2)* on myself — bunk paid, disappeared from the room's attention.

Under that cover, I locate a **framed photo of the Butcher** on the wall and pocket it while Finja keeps the barkeeper occupied.

- *She also makes out with him. Commitment to the bit.*

Cross-referencing what we learn here with what Asher later confirms: the Butcher's real name is **Albert Hoffman**. He is apparently a long-standing chapter leader for this gang.

---

## Convergence

As the situation clearly became too exposed for two people to handle alone, I flagged Thea. **Shamus** used **Portal Passage** *(Wayfare 3)* to bring **Thea** and **Asher** directly to our location — they arrive roughly half an hour after us.

**Asher** talks to people hanging around outside:

- They confirm Hoffman was present here before leaving to pull off the event from last session
- He's gone, but we now have a face, a real name, and a social network around him

---

## Grandeur

With the full group present, time to stop being subtle.

I drop **Veiled Eyes**, step into the center of the room, and cast **Grandeur** *(Sovereign 2)* — filling the space, making myself impossible to ignore, and bending the room's attention toward me.

- **Roll**: Strong success
- The men compete for the honor of giving me what I want — a few of them literally fight each other
- The winner gets a fake number; I get **Albert Hoffman's home address**
- **Shamus** sows enough discord on the way out that they're left bickering among themselves before anyone thinks to question what just happened

---

## The House

The address leads to a quiet suburb. Nice house, manicured lawn — very HOA. It fits the article Thea found earlier.

We circle to the back. Middle of the night.

**Shamus** warps the group through a solid wall via **Portal Passage** *(Wayfare 3)*.

**Thea** picks up the sound of snoring from the master bedroom. Inside: a woman, asleep.

**Albert Hoffman is married.**

*Uncertainty: kith, kinain, or mundane?*

---

## The Cellar

While the rest of us located the bedroom, **Shamus** broke off to scout the basement — Hoffman's tool room and workspace.

**What he found:**

- **7 Dross** — stored, characteristically, in the form of beef jerky
- **2 chimerical cleavers**
- **1 chimerical butcher knife**

The man commits to a theme.

> [!info] OOC
> Shamus actually found **18 dross**. The group was informed of 7. Normally a Asher move.

---

## The Interrogation

**The Plan**: Finja and Shamus hold and wake her. I take point.

She wakes up fighting — kicks Shamus for actual damage and immediately threatens us: *her husband will kill us all.* She knows exactly what kind of man she's married to.

She turns out to be a **Satyr** — not mundane, not kinain. Fully fae, fully aware.

My approach of being reasonable gets me nowhere. **Asher** takes over the interrogation.

**Result**: We have it — **Albert Hoffman's lake house**. A holiday property, outside the city. That's where he's gone.

---

## SESSION END

*The Butcher has a name, a face, and now an address. He doesn't know we're coming.*
