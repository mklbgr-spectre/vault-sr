## Following the Trail

We decided to visit relevant punk venues, cross-referencing with Shameus's knowledge of the hooligan scene. We narrowed it down to one location (more or less randomly chosen among three options).

**Inside the Venue**:

- A Troll and a Nocker who appear to be the bar operators
- They approached us territorially (fair enough—we arrived as a suspiciously large kith group, and they still have their teeth)

**Initial Tension**: Shameus and Sigrund took time to establish rapport. I couldn't help—as a Sidhe, I'd be counterproductive if these were Ranters.

**Eventually**: They warmed up and seemed quite friendly. They at least knew Rot-Face from his music performances.

**Key Information**:

- They've had Ranters at the venue before but didn't want them and kicked them out
- **Next step suggestion**: Head to the Boston harbor area and check relevant venues there for a better chance of getting closer to the Ranters
