## The Harbor Investigation

**Wednesday Evening** We reconvened and let the fae hound scent the shadow wolf's heart. It led us toward the harbor, specifically to a warehouse:

**Harbor Storage Limited**

The facility was operational—trucks actively loading and unloading cargo. Asher used social engineering to gain entry and observed the operations. He got the distinct impression that something more than routine business was happening here.

While Asher was inside, the troll went to get fast food, and the rest of us circled the perimeter, discovering a fire exit.

**Breaking In** Asher performed a security system check, slipped into the office, and cut the power. We entered quickly, aware that the security company would likely respond to the disabled cameras.

**Discoveries Inside:**

- A ritual circle—possibly the site where the hound was summoned
- A trapdoor leading into the Near Dreaming
- The same forest we'd encountered before; the wolf was apparently set on us from this location

The fae hound picked up another trail, and we're now concerned there may be more wolves...
