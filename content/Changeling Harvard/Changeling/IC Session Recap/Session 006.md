# Wolf Hunt & Mysterious Sister

## The Battle

We engaged three shadow wolves in combat. The fight was brutal:

- **Asher** was reduced to 1 HP and went down
- **Shameus** took only minor damage
- **Victory**: We defeated all three wolves and looted three more shadow hearts

---

## Discovery in the Bushes

In the aftermath, we spotted the woman from Thea's vision hiding in the bushes. She resembled me (Mira) but wore **House Ailil** clothing—a crucial detail.

Afterward, we found a black piece of fabric, presumably torn from this woman's robe.

---

We traveled back to our freehold (The Crimson Spire) through the Near Dreaming to:

- Get Asher healed
- Report to my mentor, Count Edevan

**Count Edevan's Response:**

- Healed Asher
- Dispatched his sheriff, Brychan ap Gwydion, to patrol the Near Dreaming
