## Birthrights and Frailties
- **Unearthly Beauty:**  
  +2 Appearance, even if this raises the rating above 5.  
  When invoking Wyrd, observers must roll Willpower (Diff 8).  
  Failure: awestruck 1 turn per Appearance dot.

- **Noble Bearing:**  
  Immune to cantrip humiliation.  
  Cannot botch Etiquette or Politics rolls.

- **Adoration:**  
  Mortals interacting meaningfully must roll Willpower (Diff = Glamour).  
  Failure: overly interested.  
  Botch: obsessive fixation (affection or hostility).
