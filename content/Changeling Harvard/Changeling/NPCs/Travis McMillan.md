# Travis McMillan / "Rot-Face"
**Kith:** Redcap | **Status:** Deceased

Hardcore punk singer and Ranter sympathizer, murdered at the Crimson Spire during the Halloween night of 2025.

## Identity
- Stage name / fae nickname: "Rot-Face"
- Singer in a hardcore punk band (not just a fan)
- Deeply anti-noble; particularly hated Sidhe
- Had contact with the [[The Butcher|Ranters]], apparently reluctantly: *"Made contact — the fucking assholes but I need them"*
- Mentioned [[Arwynn]] by name in his notebook with particular hostility

## Belongings (found in apartment safe)
- Beretta with two magazines
- Mason jar filled with plectrums (hardcore punk concert souvenirs)
- First-edition hardcore punk vinyl record
- Notebook with the above entries
- 3 Dross (divided among the group)

## Appearances
- **Session 7:** Discovered dead in front of the Crimson Spire storage cellar the morning of November 1st. Cause of death: dagger to the head plus cut wounds. The dagger belonged to [[Arwynn]], though he is not believed to be the killer. Sigrund had matching cut wounds — she was apparently attacked by the same perpetrator.

## Notes
- The murder weapon (Arwynn's dagger) and Rot-Face's own notebook connections make this complicated
- His punk band connections were a lead toward the Ranters; venue staff confirmed he performed there
