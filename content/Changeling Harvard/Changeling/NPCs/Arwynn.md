# Arwynn
**Kith:** Sidhe | **House:** Fiona | **Court:** Unseelie | **Title:** Knight

## Identity
- Knight of House Fiona
- Unseelie, with a pronounced air of smug amusement
- Has enemies described by Geoffrey as "angry husbands"
- His dagger was the murder weapon in the Rot-Face case — but we do not believe he is the killer

## Appearances
- **Session 2:** Made his first appearance just before the group executed the Handsome Dan heist. Announced he had placed a $500 bet on their success. Reappeared at the pub afterward to collect, presumably.
- **Session 5:** Received a letter from [[Elly ap Fiona]], delivered via Asher.
- **Session 7:** Found at the Crimson Spire Halloween bonfire with Mira's makeup smeared on his face and chest pain. Mira revived him with chest compressions and mouth-to-mouth. His dagger was found at the Rot-Face murder scene.

## Notes
- Close enough to the Crimson Spire to be present at major events, but politically Unseelie — his exact relationship to Edevan's court is ambiguous
- The connection between Elly ap Fiona's letter and whatever Arwynn is involved in has not been followed up
