# Unknown Woman in House Ailil Robes
**Kith:** Unknown | **House:** Ailil (based on clothing) | **Status:** Unidentified

A recurring figure in visions and one confirmed in-person sighting. May be connected to Mira's past fae life.

## Known Details
- Resembles Mira physically
- Wears House Ailil robes (black fabric)
- Linked to the shadow wolves via Thea's omen
- Left behind a torn piece of black fabric after the second wolf fight

## Appearances
- **Session 5 (omen):** Thea's Soothsay reading connected the wolves to a woman — and pointed generally toward Boston (Unseelie court territory)
- **Session 6:** Spotted hiding in the bushes after the three-wolf fight in the Near Dreaming. Fled before contact could be made. The torn black fabric was recovered from the scene.

## Investigative Thread
- Count Edevan cast Soothsay on the fabric repeatedly — results were inconsistent, but when they worked, **they pointed to Mira**
- Edevan asked Mira about fae siblings; she rolled 2 successes on Remembrance and recalled using the word "sister" in her previous life
- [[Elly ap Fiona]] at the Boston freehold bears a notable resemblance to this woman — connection unresolved
- The scrying result implicating Mira may indicate a past-life bond rather than direct guilt

## Notes
- Possible identity: Mira's fae sister from her previous incarnation
- House Ailil is traditionally Unseelie and politically opposed to House Eiluned — the contrast is charged
