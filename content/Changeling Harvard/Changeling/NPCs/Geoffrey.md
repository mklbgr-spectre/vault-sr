# Geoffrey
**Role:** Pub barkeeper at the Crimson Spire freehold

## Identity
- Staff member and barkeeper at the Crimson Spire's in-house pub
- Recurring contact for the group

## Appearances
- **Session 7:** Interviewed during the Rot-Face murder investigation. Knew nothing about Rot-Face's enemies. On Arwynn's enemies: "angry husbands." Not a useful lead, but cooperative.
- **Session 9:** Joined the group's pursuit of the Ranters on the highway in a pickup truck. Active participant in the operation.

## Notes
- More than just a barkeep — willing to get involved when it counts
