## Merits and Flaws

Many Merits and Flaws don’t make sense for Shinma because they are unlike the Kithain in so many ways. Surreal Quality, Iron Allergy, Chimerical Magnet, Faerie Eternity, Poetic Heart, Cleared Mists, Chimerical Disability, Seeming’s Blessing, Slipped Seeming, and Sidhe’s Curse are not appropriate for Hsien characters.
