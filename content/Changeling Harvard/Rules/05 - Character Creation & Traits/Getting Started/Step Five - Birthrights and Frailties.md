Every kith has unique benefits, known as Birthrights, as
well as inherited drawbacks, known as Frailties. You should
look over these Traits carefully, as they often add new systems
to your character, or change existing Trait values. For instance,
sidhe characters receive a bonus to their Appearance Trait to
reflect their otherworldly beauty, while boggans cannot botch
any rolls involving the Crafts ability. Bonuses gained from
Birthrights may take your character over five dots, granting
truly superhuman levels of that Trait.
