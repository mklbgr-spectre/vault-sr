Out of all the terrors the Dreaming spawns, Dauntain are
among the most frightening. A changeling may fear rampaging
chimera, the Shadow Court fomenting discord, or society laid to
waste by the Thallain, but these are, in essence, mortal terrors.
Fear of the Dauntain is a horror that chills straight to the soul.
Dauntain changelings are things that should not be, but
somehow are. Most changelings believe that when they die,
they are born anew, washed clean of their former sins and
failures with a brand new Chrysalis. It is a comforting story,
but it is not always true.
Some fae awaken wrong. Sometimes, the Chrysalis twists
and claws, leaving the fae soul scarred and the human mind
broken. Sometimes, the soul was damaged long before its rein-
carnation, bringing its scars and fractures through the cycle of
rebirth. The Dreaming should have destroyed these hopelessly
damaged souls, but instead spits them back out into the world,
incomplete and deranged.
