## Mortal Identity

Investigative reporter — Seattle based, probably freelance or mid-tier local press. His job is noticing things that don't add up and asking questions until they do. That's also exactly what the Chrysalis is doing to him.

## Play Notes

Face playstyle — high Social, reads people and rooms. Central theme is identity and belonging; the Chrysalis is framed as amnesia, so he is actively piecing together who he is during the session. His mortal instinct to investigate and the fae awakening are pulling in the same direction — he can't stop noticing things.

Useful for introducing new players to the setting: he has an in-world reason to ask questions and react with fresh eyes.
