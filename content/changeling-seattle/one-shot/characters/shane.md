## Mortal Identity

One of the very few Pankration instructors in the US — runs a small dojo in Seattle. Pankration is ancient Greek full-contact combat, no rules, everything is a weapon. Niche enough that it turns heads; serious enough that nobody questions her credentials once they've seen her move.

## Play Notes

Direct translation from Shadowrun, no adjustment needed. Passionate, physical, driven by appetite and intensity. The Satyr kith boon ties naturally into her existing character energy — lean into that in play.

The dojo is hers — her space, her passion, her livelihood. That ownership matters to her the same way Marla's alley matters to Marla.
