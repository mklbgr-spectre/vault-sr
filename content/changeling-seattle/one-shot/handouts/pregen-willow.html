Cantrips
Sparrows and Nightingales (Metamorphosis 1)
Change one feature of target — hair, voice, height, gender, object material. Must be a plausible alternative. Duration by successes: 1=scene, 2=day, 3=Glamour in days, 4=month, 5=year. Can attach a condition to end the effect early.

Worms and Giants (Metamorphosis 2)
Shrink or enlarge target. 1 success = ¾ or 1½× size; up to 1/16 or 3½× at 5 successes. Lasts one scene. Castings stack. Type: Wyrd.

Trick of the Light (Chicanery 1)
Viewers misinterpret target for 1 turn per success — wrong face, wrong voice, wrong object. Falls apart under scrutiny (Perception + Occult diff 8 after 1 turn).
Kith: Fox Pooka
Boon — Animal Form: Shift into fox form, full or partial. Fox senses and movement apply. Metamorphosis cantrips layer on top — Sparrows and Nightingales can alter the fox form itself.

Frailty — Compulsive Lying: Cannot speak a direct, literal truth. Must always shade, omit, or mislead — even when it hurts.

  • Has been coming to Rainier Lanes since her teens. Marla has known her longer than most people have known her at all.
