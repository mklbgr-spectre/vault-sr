Cantrips
Protocol (Sovereign 1)
All present must observe Kithain etiquette. Violations — even unintentional — cost dice equal to cantrip successes on all pools for the rest of the scene. Any changeling of higher rank than Hamish can spend Willpower to ignore it. Type: Wyrd.

Grandeur (Sovereign 2)
Others perceive caster as royalty or natural leader. Social rolls gain automatic successes equal to cantrip successes. Affected individuals must spend Willpower to contradict, accost, or attack. Lasts one scene — ends immediately if publicly humiliated. Type: Wyrd.

Done Deal (Contract 1)
Seals a formal agreement with Glamour. Oathbreakers suffer automatic botches equal to successes, at the most dramatically ironic moments. Can sanctify oaths between others as witness. Requires a genuine formal promise — casual agreements don't qualify. Type: Chimerical.
Kith: Sidhe — House Ailil
Boon — Noble Bearing: In fae mien, presence commands awe. Mortals and changelings of lesser Title must make Willpower rolls to act against or dismiss in formal situations. May add Glamour as bonus dice to Appearance-based rolls in full fae mien.

Frailty — Cold Iron: Takes aggravated damage from cold iron. Even brief contact is acutely painful.

Flaw — Spirit Pariah: Chimeras and chimerical creatures instinctively distrust or avoid him. His Ailil bloodline reads as threat to wild fae things. This cannot be argued away.

Who You Are in Seattle
  • Holds a minor title in the Ailil court — "Sir Hamish" in formal contexts. Nobody here uses it. He hasn't corrected them.
  • Mortal cover: independent financial consultant. Credible enough that nobody asks what he actually does.
  • Came to Rainier Lanes because he needed somewhere the court didn't follow him. Found it. Keeps coming back.
  • Wild fae things don't trust him on instinct. Mortals often do. He finds this genuinely surprising every time.
