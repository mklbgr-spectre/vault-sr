Cantrips
Trick of the Light (Chicanery 1)
Viewers misinterpret target for 1 turn per success — wrong face, voice, or object. Anyone paying close attention for more than a turn can roll Perception + Occult (diff 8) to see through it. Type: Chimerical.

Veiled Eyes (Chicanery 2)
Target becomes forgettable — not invisible, just not worth noticing. Duration: 1=turn, 2=minute, 3=hour, 4=scene, 5=day. Extra Realms create exceptions. Resisted by Perception + Kenning (diff 8) if actively searching. Type: Chimerical or Wyrd.

Omen (Soothsay 1)
Brief vague glimpse of a target's destiny thread. By successes: 1=immediate plans, 2=court/nature, 3=long-term goal, 4=inner nature/Legacy, 5=closely-guarded secret. Always subject to interpretation. Repeated casts on the same target cost +1 Glamour each. Type: Chimerical.
Kith: Piskie
Boon — Wings: In fae mien, can fly at running speed. Also supernaturally nimble — add Glamour to Dexterity rolls to squeeze through tight spaces, escape holds, or move through obstacles.

Frailty — Compulsive Curiosity: Cannot ignore an unsolved puzzle, mystery, or person who doesn't add up without a Willpower roll (difficulty 7).
