Dice & Rolls
Common pools — for Shadowrun players

Roll: Attribute + Ability. Count dice that meet or beat the difficulty (default 6). Each qualifying die = 1 success.

Rolled 1s subtract from your successes. Zero successes = failure. At least one 1 and no net successes = botch.

1 — barely 3 — solid 5+ — exceptional botch — something interesting happens

Perception + Alertness Diff 6. Passive; GM calls for it.

Perception + Empathy Diff 6. Mood, intent, emotional state.

Perception + Subterfuge Diff 6. Opposed by their Manipulation + Subterfuge.

Dexterity + Stealth Opposed by Perception + Alertness of anyone watching.

Combat & Cantrips
Common pools — for Shadowrun players
Combat
Attack (weapon) SR: Weapon skill
Dexterity + Melee Diff 6. Defender rolls Dexterity + Dodge (diff 6) — successes cancel yours.
Attack (unarmed) SR: Unarmed Combat
Strength + Brawl Diff 6. Same dodge opposition applies.
Soak damage SR: Body
Stamina Diff 6 (bashing) / Diff 8 (lethal). Each success removes one health level of damage.
Resist mental / social effects SR: Composure / Resistance
Willpower Difficulty set by the effect. Also spent as a flat point to gain an automatic success on any roll (once per roll).
How Damage Works

1. Attack lands — net successes on your attack roll add to your damage dice pool.
2. Roll damage — weapon rating + net attack successes, diff 6. Successes = health levels dealt.
3. Defender soaks — Stamina roll, diff 6 (bashing) / diff 8 (lethal). Each success removes one health level.
Unarmed = Strength dice, bashing. Weapons = Strength + bonus, lethal.

Cantrips — Fae Magic
Roll Art + lowest Realm used, difficulty 8. Chimerical cantrips cost no Glamour. Wyrd costs 1 Glamour. Extra costs only from modifications (additional Realms, etc.). Successes determine effect strength and duration.
Realm required
Every cantrip needs a Realm that covers the target — Actor for people, Fae for changelings, Nature for animals/plants, Prop for objects. Your Realm rating must be ≥ 1 to target that category. Check your sheet. If your Realm is 0 for that target type, you can't cast on it.
Chimerical vs. Wyrd
Chimerical — only fae and enchanted people perceive the effect. Mortals see nothing.
Wyrd — everyone sees it. Mortals rationalise it. Costs +1 Glamour. Each cantrip on your sheet is labelled.
The Bunk
Before casting, you can perform a bunk — a brief ritual that channels Glamour. You describe it; the GM assigns bonus dice based on how elaborate, creative, or risky it is. −1 — a gesture or single word  |  −2 — a short phrase, a specific action  |  −3 — conspicuous (singing, spinning, a rhyme out loud)  |  −4 / −5 — time-consuming, self-endangering, or completely outrageous The bunk is not flavour — it is mechanics. Diff 8 is brutal. Make it yours.
