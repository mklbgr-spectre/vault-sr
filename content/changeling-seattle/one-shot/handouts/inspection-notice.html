Re:  Scheduled Premises Inspection — Noise Complaint Follow-Up / General Compliance Review

This notice is to inform you that the Seattle Department of Construction and Inspections will conduct a scheduled compliance inspection of the above-referenced premises on the date indicated. An authorized representative of the property must be present and available for the duration of the inspection.

This inspection has been initiated following receipt of formal noise complaints filed under Seattle Municipal Code 25.08 (Noise Control Ordinance). In addition to addressing the cited complaint(s), inspectors will conduct a general compliance review of the premises in accordance with SMC Title 22 (Building Code) and applicable fire and safety regulations.

The inspection will include but may not be limited to:

  • Exterior and common areas, including adjacent parking and access points
  • Structural condition and general building maintenance
  • Electrical systems, panels, and wiring compliance
  • Fire safety equipment, suppression systems, and egress routes
  • Ventilation, plumbing, and mechanical systems
  • Compliance with applicable ADA accessibility requirements

Failure to provide access to all areas of the premises, including maintenance and utility spaces, may constitute obstruction of a municipal inspection and may result in the issuance of a Notice of Violation, civil penalties under SMC 22.808.050, and referral for further enforcement action.

Please contact this office at (206) 684-8600 referencing the case number above to confirm receipt of this notice and to address any scheduling concerns. All written correspondence must include the case number.
