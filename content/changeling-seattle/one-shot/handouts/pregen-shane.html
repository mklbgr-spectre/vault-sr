Cantrips
Hopscotch (Wayfare 1)
Prodigious leap — caster or target. Landing always safe. By successes: 1=1 story/30ft, 2=2 stories/60ft, 3=5 stories/150ft, 4=10 stories/300ft, 5=as far as the eye can see. Objects leap wildly; resist with Str + Athletics (diff 7). Type: Wyrd.

Quicksilver (Wayfare 2)
Each success = one extra action or double movement on next turn. Split between the two as desired. Hurled objects add successes as bonus damage dice. Type: Wyrd.

Sparrows and Nightingales (Metamorphosis 1)
Change one physical feature — height, voice, hair, gender, material. Must be a plausible alternative. Duration: 1=scene, 2=day, 3=Glamour in days, 4=month, 5=year.
Kith: Satyr
Boon — Passion: Sense strong emotions in others. Gain Glamour from witnessing or inspiring passionate acts. Social rolls to inspire or provoke add bonus dice equal to Empathy.

Frailty — Cannot Refuse a Challenge: Cannot decline a direct personal challenge to skill or courage without a Willpower roll (difficulty 8).
