# One-Shot Pregens

Translated from Shadowrun 5 characters for the system demo. These are not campaign characters — clean separation applies after the one-shot.

---

## Willow — Fox Pooka

**Source character:** Willow (Shadowrun)
**Kith:** Pooka (fox)

**Concept:** The shapeshifter with a kind heart and a trickster's instincts. Passionate and protective, but constitutionally incapable of telling the whole truth.

**Key mechanic to flag at session zero:** The Pooka compulsive lying flaw is hard-coded — she *cannot* speak a direct truth. Work out with the player how she communicates around it (misdirection, half-truths, truths framed as jokes).

**Kith birthright/frailty:** Shapeshifting into her animal form; the lying compulsion.

---

## Fariel — Piskie

**Source character:** Fariel (Shadowrun)
**Kith:** Piskie

**Concept:** A face who fits in everywhere — but is that skill, or is it blood? The identity question is the character. Emotional detachment that presents as social ease; memories before university are hazy (read as Chrysalis confusion).

**Key mechanic:** Piskie birthright — naturally belongs wherever they are, people accept their presence without question. Plays into the undercover/infiltration playstyle.

**Thematic hook:** Is he fitting in because he chose to, or because his kith compels it? The one-shot doesn't need to answer this — just surface it.

**Note:** Sluagh was considered but ruled out (cannot speak above a whisper — too limiting for a face player). Eiluned Sidhe was considered but Piskie fits the playstyle more cleanly.

---

## Hamish — Sidhe

**Source character:** Hamish (Shadowrun)
**Kith:** Sidhe

**Concept:** Believes in his own noble bloodline — and in Changeling, that belief is structurally correct. The Shadowrun "Spirit Pariah" curse translates as chimera being instinctively wary of him: the outsider feeling is preserved but reframed.

**Arts focus:** Sovereign (barrier creation / defensive magic maps cleanly).

**Key mechanic to flag:** Sidhe carry the weight of Arcadian origin and the Accordance War's aftermath. Other Kithain may have complicated feelings about Sidhe, especially commoner kith.

---

## Shane — Satyr

**Source character:** Shane (Shadowrun)
**Kith:** Satyr

**Concept:** Direct translation — combat focus, passion, directness. She already is one. The father's shadow backstory is pure character colour, needs no mechanical anchor.

**Key mechanic:** Satyr Birthright grants bonus dice to athletics and passion-driven actions. Natural Glamour generator in performance/intensity contexts.
