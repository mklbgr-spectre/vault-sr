## Empire of the Firebird

Before the fall of the Soviet Union, contact with [[Changeling|changelings]] in those lands did not exist except for rumors, few of them good. Since the demise of the Soviet state, those lands have opened up to communication with the west. Recently, a contingent from House [[Varich]] has traveled to the homeland of their dreams, determined to reawaken the silenced voices of the leshi, vodyanoi, berenginy, bannik, kikimora, domovoi, dvorovoi, polevik, and other native spirits, and to restore [[Glamour]] to a barren land (or so they see it). The Empire of the Firebird has established itself in Moscow, where Tzar Ivan and Tzarina Ekaterina rule from their Kremlin-style [[Freeholds|freehold]], the Winter Dream. Whether or not they succeed in bringing other Russian [[Changeling|changelings]] into their dream remains undecided.
