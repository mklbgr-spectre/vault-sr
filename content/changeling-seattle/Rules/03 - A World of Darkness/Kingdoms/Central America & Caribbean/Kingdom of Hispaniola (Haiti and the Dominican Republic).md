The changelings of Hispaniola live almost exclusively within the Dominican Republic, which occupies most of the island. Don Enrico Fernandez, a [[Troll]] born in the Dominican Republic, inherited the rule of the kingdom after the death of his father. He rules a group of native-born and émigré commoners and nobles in relative peace. They guard their border with Haiti for fear of the strange [[01 Chimera]] that often try to cross into the Dominican Republic.
