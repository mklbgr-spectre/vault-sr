To stay alive and maintain her fae soul, a changeling must find sources of [[Glamour]] to keep herself alive. One way to seek out [[Glamour]] is to find mortals whose creativity and dreams naturally create [[Glamour]] and gather it from them. Another possibility is to find places rich with the stuff to stay protected from the Banal world. Such locations include [[Glades]], [[Freeholds]], and [[03 - A World of Darkness/Places of Glamour/Trods]].
