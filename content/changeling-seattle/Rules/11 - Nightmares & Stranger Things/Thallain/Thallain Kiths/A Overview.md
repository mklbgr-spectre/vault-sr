Thallain Kiths

Following are 19 Thallain kiths, some of them corresponding
to their Seelie and Unseelie cousins, and some that are unique
to the walking nightmares. The kith writeups present enough
information and game mechanics for the Storyteller to use them
as antagonists, but if your troupe leans towards darker games,
nothing prevents you from taking on the role of a Thallain in
a Changeling chronicle.
