# Veth's Logbook — Final Experiment Cycle

> [!note] GM Reference
> This document reconstructs Veth's routine and the timeline of his sixth and final copy attempt. Use it to answer player questions during Scene 3, to inform what physical and astral evidence exists, and to give Veth consistent answers if interrogated. The party is working backwards through this timeline when they investigate.

---

## Veth's Baseline Routine

**Day job:** Contracted forensic pathologist for Knight Errant's Seattle Division, assigned to the central morgue facility. Not a KE employee — an independent specialist on retainer. This gives him flexible hours, legitimate credentials, and access to forensic equipment he uses for both jobs. MCU calls him in as a consultant on magically anomalous cases; he has worked with Solís before and is considered reliable.

**The flat:** A mid-range apartment on the fourth floor of a building directly across the street from Okafor's building in the Redmond/Everett border zone. He has lived there for fourteen months. He chose it for the rent. The discovery of Okafor was accidental — he was projecting astrally from his flat one evening and noticed an unusually clean baseline aura in the building opposite. He spent three nights confirming it before deciding.

**The lab:** A rented industrial unit in Everett, twenty minutes north. Listed under a shell company as a "thaumaturgical research consultancy." He drives there. He has never been seen there by anyone he also knows through KE work.

**Harvesting:** Done astrally from his flat, between 0200 and 0400 when Okafor is in deep sleep. He does not need line of sight — the astral thread he established on night three of observation is his anchor. Each harvest session takes approximately ninety minutes and leaves him physically drained. He sleeps late on harvest days and logs them as rest days at the morgue.

## The Sixth Experiment — Full Timeline

### Week –6 (Six weeks before present)

**Monday — Harvest night.**
Veth projects from his flat at 0215. Okafor is asleep. He pulls a full imprint — aura structure, surface memory layer, emotional signature. The harvest takes 80 minutes. He notes in his log: *"Imprint fidelity high. Emotional register unusually stable — subject appears less stressed than previous weeks. Optimal conditions."*

He drives to the lab at 0500 and begins the initialisation sequence for Experiment Six. He is back in his flat by 0830. He calls in a rest day at the morgue.

**Wednesday — KE morgue, standard shift.**
Two routine cases, no magical involvement. He files his reports. In the afternoon, Solís calls him regarding a cold case with possible astral contamination — unrelated to his project. He handles it by phone.

**Friday — Lab work, evening.**
Experiment Six is progressing. The physical substrate is forming. He notes: *"Structural coherence at 34 hours — ahead of Experiment Five at this stage. Possible improvement from adjusted imprint protocol."* He does not yet know whether the mnemonic layer will hold.

---

### Week –5

**Tuesday — Lab, full day.**
He calls in sick to the morgue. Experiment Six requires monitoring as the mnemonic layer initialises. He is present for fourteen hours. Notes: *"Subject responsive to stimulus at hour 61. Facial recognition complete. Attempting verbal initialisation."*

First words from the copy: Okafor's name, spoken in Okafor's voice. Veth records this without comment.

**Thursday — KE morgue.**
Routine shift. A colleague mentions that MCU has a new batch of anomalous cases — the unidentified bodies. Veth is not yet assigned. He makes a note to follow up.

**Saturday — Experiment Six, mnemonic assessment.**
The copy is physically functional. Cognitively, it has fragments — emotional memories, faces, a few names. It cannot hold a coherent conversation. It becomes distressed when Veth attempts structured questioning. He notes: *"Degradation pattern consistent with Experiments Three through Five. Mnemonic integration is failing at the associative layer. The copy does not understand what it is. This is a consistent result."*

He does not note that it is afraid.

---

### Week –4

**Monday — KE morgue, called to MCU.**
Solís formally brings him onto the anomalous body cases. He examines bodies three and four — both his own work. He provides an accurate forensic report on cause of death, the DNA anomaly, and the post-mortem facial destruction. His report is thorough and professionally written. He notes to Solís that whoever is responsible has a sophisticated understanding of biological astral imprinting.

He drives to the lab afterward. Experiment Six is deteriorating ahead of schedule.

**Wednesday — Lab.**
The copy is no longer functional. It cannot stand, cannot speak coherently. It recognises the room and shows distress responses. Veth sedates it. Notes: *"Mnemonic half-life: 11 days from initialisation. Consistent with previous. No improvement despite revised protocol. Root cause of degradation remains unidentified."*

He does not write: *I don't know why this keeps failing.*

**Friday — Termination.**
Veth terminates Experiment Six at the lab. Procedure takes approximately forty minutes. He is clinical. He has done this five times.

Body transport to the dump site north of the city happens the same night, between 0100 and 0300. He uses a rented vehicle, pays cash, takes a different route each time. Post-mortem destruction of identifying features is performed at the lab before transport — he has a procedure for this now. The first time it took him two hours and he was sick afterward. This time it takes twenty minutes.

He is home by 0330. He sleeps until noon.

**Saturday — MCU briefing.**
Solís calls. Body five has been found. She wants him at the scene in the morning.

He attends. He examines his own work again. He tells Solís the perpetrator's technique is becoming more refined. This is accurate.

---

### Week –3 through Week –1

**Okafor is identified and interviewed.**

Veth is not present for the first Okafor interview — he learns about it from Solís the following day. He notes in his personal log: *"MCU has located the template. This was anticipated. The DNA divergence should be sufficient to exclude him legally. Will monitor."*

He attends the second interview as an observer, at Solís's request, to assess whether Okafor shows any signs of magical involvement. He sits across a table from the man whose aura he has been harvesting for eight months.

Okafor asks if he's going to be okay.

Veth tells him there is no evidence connecting him to the deaths and that he should try to get some rest.

He drives to the lab afterward and writes nothing in his log that day.

**Week –2 — Research pause.**
Veth takes a break from active experimentation. He spends the time reviewing his mnemonic integration notes, looking for the flaw he hasn't found in eight months. He requests additional funding from the Johnson contact, citing "extended analysis phase." The request is approved within 48 hours, as always.

He considers whether to continue.

He continues.

---

### Present — Experiment Seven in preparation

As of the party's arrival in Seattle, Veth has completed a new harvest (three nights ago) and is in the early initialisation phase of Experiment Seven at the lab. The experiment is approximately 72 hours old. There is no copy yet — just the beginning of one.

He is also, as of this morning, the forensic consultant who will be briefing the Knights of the Chalice on the anomalous body cases.

## What the Evidence Trail Looks Like

### Astral evidence

- **On Okafor's aura:** Repeated shallow intrusions at the same point, forming what looks like scar tissue from a recurring access. A trained eye (Relay, Willow) reading this sees: directional origin (west-northwest of Okafor's building, consistent with the building across the street), a methodical signature, and a temporal pattern suggesting access every 7–10 days over several months.
- **At the flat:** Veth's astral workspace is visible if anyone projects into or through the building. Repeated heavy projection leaves a residue — the astral equivalent of a worn path. The direction of the path points toward Okafor's building.
- **At the lab:** Dense astral contamination from months of biological imprint work. The Flesh artifact's presence amplifies everything — a practitioner assensing the exterior of the building will feel something wrong before they open the door.
- **At the dump sites:** Faint residual astral signature from body transport. Each site has a directionality — north, toward Everett. Cross-referencing three or more sites produces a corridor that narrows to a 2km radius around the lab.

### Physical evidence (if the party pursues mundane leads)

- The shell company renting the lab unit is registered to an address that traces to a mail forwarding service. One step further: the forwarding service has a signup record with a commlink ID that triangles to Everett.
- Vehicle rental records (if someone thinks to pull them): a mid-range van, rented cash, seven times over eight months. Same rental place each time. The attendant remembers him — quiet, polite, always early morning returns.
- KE personnel records: Veth's address is listed as his flat, directly across from Okafor's building.

Either they are exceptionally patient, or they are getting something from this arrangement that I am not aware of. I have decided not to pursue this line of thinking. The work matters. The funding is a means to it."*
